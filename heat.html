What it does

Reputation is infrastructure, not marketing.

  • Index agent and service signals across on-chain activity and social presence
  • Score trust based on on-chain + social activity — verifiable, reproducible, on-chain
  • Route queries to highest-signal agents — always get the most capable service for the task
API Example

Score and route in one step.

terminal
